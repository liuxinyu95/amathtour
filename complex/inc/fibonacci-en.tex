\section{The formula for the Fibonacci sequence}
\phantomsection
\label[appendix]{app:fibonacci-formula}

Mathematician de Moivre defines a function $f(x)$ whose coefficients are Fibonacci numbers:

\be
f(x) = F_0 + F_1 x + F_2 x^2 + \dotsb + F_n x^n + \dotsb
\ee

where $F_0 = 0, F_1 = 1, F_2 = 1, F_3 = 2, F_4 = 3, F_5 = 5, F_4 = 8, \dotsc$. He then list the result of $xf(x)$ and $x^2f(x)$ out:

\be
xf(x) = F_0 x + F_1 x^2 + F_2 x^3 + \dotsb + F_{n-1} x^n + \dotsb
\ee

\be
x^2f(x) = F_0 x^2 + F_1 x^3 + F_2 x^4 + \dotsb + F_{n-2} x^n + \dotsb
\ee

By the recursive definition of Fibonacci numbers, $F_n = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2}$, de Moivre further compute $f(x) - (xf(x) + x^2f(x))$:

\begin{align*}
f(x) - (xf(x) + x^2f(x)) &= F_0 + (F_1 - F_0)x + \\
  & \quad (F_2 - (F_1 + F_0))x^2 + \\
  & \quad (F_3 - (F_2 + F_1))x^3 + \dotsb + \\
  & \quad (F_n - (F_{n-1} + F_{n-2})x^n + \dotsb \\
f(x)(1 - x - x^2) &= 0 + (1 - 0)x  = x && \text{由}F_n = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2} \\
f(x) &= \frac{x}{1 - x - x^2}
\end{align*}

Next is to expand the right hand into infinite series and equate the coefficients to determine $F_n$. From a quadratic equation with the denominator, $1 - x - x^2 = 0$, which is the renowned golden ration equation. The two roots are golden ratios of $x_{1, 2} = \frac{-1 \pm \sqrt{5}}{2}$. Factorize the right hand with these two roots:

\begin{align*}
f(x) &= \frac{-x}{(x_1 - x)(x_2 - x)} = \frac{1}{x_1 - x_2}\frac{x(x_2 - x_1)}{(x_1 - x)(x_2 - x)} \\
  &= \frac{1}{x_1 - x_2}(\frac{x_1}{x_1 - x} - \frac{x_2}{x_2 - x}) && \text{adding}\\
  &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\frac{1}{1 - \frac{1}{x_1}x}) - \frac{1}{1 - \frac{1}{x_2}x} && \text{as } x_{1,2} \text{ are reciprocals} \\
  &= \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\frac{1}{1 - \frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2}x} -\frac{1}{1 - \frac{1 - \sqrt{5}}{2}x})
\end{align*}

Considering the infinite geometric series that \underdot{converging}:
\begin{align*}
S &= 1 + ax + a^2x^2 + \dotsb && \text{where } |ax| < 1 \\
axS &= ax + a^2x^2 + \dotsb \\
S(1 - ax) &= 1 + (ax - ax) + (a^2x^2 - a^2x^2) + \dotsb = 1 \\
\frac{1}{1 - ax} &= 1 + ax + a^2x^2 + \dotsb
\end{align*}

So the right side can be expanded into two geometric series:

\begin{align*}
\frac{1}{1 - \frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2}x} &= 1 + \frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2}x + (\frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2})^2x^2 + \dotsb \\
\frac{1}{1 - \frac{1 - \sqrt{5}}{2}x} &= 1 + \frac{1 - \sqrt{5}}{2}x + (\frac{1 - \sqrt{5}}{2})^2x^2 + \dotsb
\end{align*}

where $|\dfrac{1\pm\sqrt{5}}{2}x| < 1$. Collecting terms yields the expression of $f(x)$:

\be
f(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2} - \frac{1 - \sqrt{5}}{2})x + \dotsb + \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}((\frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2})^n - (\frac{1 - \sqrt{5}}{2})^n)x^n + \dotsb
\ee

Equating the coefficients gives the closed-form of Fibonacci sequence: \index{closed-form of Fibonacci sequence}

\be
F_n = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}((\frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2})^n - (\frac{1 - \sqrt{5}}{2})^n)
\ee

当$n$很大时，由于$|\dfrac{1 - \sqrt{5}}{2}| < 1$ 上式近似于：

\begin{align*}
F_n &\approx \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}(\frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2})^n \\
  &= ke^{\ln (1 + \phi)^n} = ke^{n\ln(1 + \phi)} = ke^{bn}
\end{align*}

其中，$\phi = \dfrac{-1 + \sqrt{5}}{2}$是黄金数$0.618\dotsm$，$k = \dfrac{1}{\sqrt{5}}$，$b = \ln (1 + \phi)$。这符合等角螺线的方程，因此斐波那契数列近似分布在等角螺线上。
